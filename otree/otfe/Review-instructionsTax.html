We will "tax" you on earnings from the work tasks.

Your default tax rate will be 25%.

However, with some probability, the tax rate may change.

If the tax rate stays at 25%, then for every $1 you earn, you would keep 75 cents, and we will keep the remaining 25 cents.

After each work task, we will inform you of your:

  • initial gross earnings (before we tax them)
  • tax rate
  • after-tax earnings
