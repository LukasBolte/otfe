We want to know what taxes you expect.

Hence, we will ask you: “What is your expected tax rate for your earnings this coming work period?”

We will ask you before each work period.

If your answer is "sufficiently close" to the true average tax rate participants like yourself—who read the same instructions—face, you will be paid a bonus of ${{ C.BELIEF_BONUS }}.

By "sufficiently close" we mean within 2.5 percentage points of the true average tax rate. For example, if the true average tax rate is 25%, a "sufficiently close" response would be between 22.5% and 27.5%.
